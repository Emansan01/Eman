About Me

Hello there! I'm Emmanuel Sanchez, born in the state of Minnesota. My roots trace back to Mexico, where my family hails from. Some folks jokingly say I live under a rock because I'm not always up-to-date with the latest happenings in the world. Truth be told, I prioritize my personal space and the things directly around me. Decision-making is not my strong suit, but I'm genuinely unfazed by whatever path I end up taking. You could describe me as someone who embraces spontaneity and finds joy in the present moment.

My Interests

In the tapestry of my life, you'll find four prominent threads that color my world. Origami is my artistic refuge, where I transform simple sheets of paper into intricate creations that reflect my creativity. Traveling is my passport to discovery, allowing me to explore new landscapes, experience diverse cultures, and create lasting memories.

Music is the soundtrack to my journey, with a playlist that mirrors the eclectic nature of my tastes. From the soothing melodies that accompany quiet moments to the energetic beats that fuel my workouts at the gym, music is woven into every aspect of my life. Speaking of which, the gym is my sanctuary for physical well-being, where I channel my energy into maintaining a healthy and active lifestyle.
